# BBC

# BBC

Author: @HenryQW

Example: https://rsshub.app/bbc/world-asia

Route: /bbc/:channel?

Parameters:

  • channel, optional -

    channel, default to top stories

Provides a better reading experience (full text articles) over the official ones.

Support major channels, refer to BBC RSS feeds (opens new window). Eg, business for https://feeds.bbci.co.uk/news/business/rss.xml.

  • Channel contains sub-directories, such as https://feeds.bbci.co.uk/news/world/asia/rss.xml, replace / with -, /bbc/world-asia.

# Boston.com

# News

Author: @oppilate

Example: https://rsshub.app/boston/technology

Route: /boston/:tag?

Parameters:

  • tag, optional -

    Tag

Generates full-text feeds that the official feed doesn't provide. Refer to Boston.com's feed page (opens new window) for tags. For instance, https://www.boston.com/tag/local-news/?feed=rss corresponds to /boston/local-news.
