# Most Read & Most Share

Author: @nczitzk

Example: https://rsshub.app/cgtn/most/read/day

Route: /cgtn/most/:type?/:time?

Parameters:

  • type, optional -

    Type, read as most read, share as most share, read by default

  • time, optional -

    Time range, all as all the time, day as today, week as this week, month as this month, year as this year, all by default
